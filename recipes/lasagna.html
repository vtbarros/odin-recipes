Lasagna

Description

A tradicional italian dish composed of pasta sheets, tomato sauce and vegetables

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cucumber
  • Soy granulate
  • Bechamel sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook the sauce with tomatos
  2. Add veggies
  3. Add pasta sheets in between
  4. Add bechamel sauce
  5. Take to the oven for 20 min
